Trail Overview

Walk the Sacred Path

The Prayer Trail winds through the natural grounds of Calvary Baptist Church, inviting you to slow down, open God's Word, and encounter His presence at each station along the way.

A printed scripture guide for each station is available in the church foyer. The trail is accessible for all ages and abilities.

"Lead me in thy truth, and teach me: for thou art the God of my salvation; on thee do I wait all the day."

— Psalm 25:5
